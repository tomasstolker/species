About
=====

Questions & feedback
--------------------

*species* is maintained by Tomas Stolker (stolker@strw.leidenuniv.nl). Feel free to send an email for questions, comments, or suggestions.

Attribution
-----------

Please cite `Stolker et al. (2020) <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2020A%26A...635A.182S>`_ whenever results from *species* are used in a publication. Please also make sure to give credit the relevant articles for the use of the publicly available data by *species*.
